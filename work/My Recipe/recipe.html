Super Scrumptious Quinoa Salad

This is a super easy meal that is sure to be a hit! Perfect for those warm summer nights.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Quinoa
  • Parsley, half cup, chopped
  • 1 lemon, with zest
  • Zucchini, half cup, cubed
  • Bell pepper, half cup, cut into bite sized pieces
  • Olive oil, two tablespoons
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Optional Pine nuts, quarter cup

Directions

  1. Prepare the quinoa according to the directions on the package. While the quinoa is cooking, chop your vegetables.
  2. When the quinoa is done cooking, fluff it with a fork.
  3. Put the quinoa and chopped vegetables in a large mixing bowl.
  4. Add olive oil, 1 teaspoon lemon zest, and juice of whole lemon.
  5. Toss to coat.
  6. Add salt and pepper, according to taste.
  7. Enjoy!

This recipe is super adaptable. Try it with other ingredients, such as pomegranate seeds or radishes for an extra burst of freshness. Prefer a little more fiber? No problem! Try adding a can of drained beans into the mix. Get creative!
